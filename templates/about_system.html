About This System

Rental Management System is a lightweight yet powerful tool designed to help rental property owners or administrators manage tenants efficiently.

Core Features:

  • Receive and approve new tenant applications.
  • Add, update, or delete tenant records.
  • Record rental payments, balances, and statuses.
  • Search and filter tenants with ease.
  • View a quick summary of total tenants, pending approvals, rent defaulters, and total rent owed.
  • Automatically send approval emails to accepted tenants.
  • Provide an intuitive and user-friendly interface for easy management.
  • Enable administrators to generate reports for tracking payments and balances.

Future Enhancements:

  • Integration with digital/mobile payment gateways (e.g., M-Pesa, PayPal) for seamless payments.
  • Monthly and annual report generation, allowing property owners to track trends and forecast future rents.
  • Support for managing multiple rental properties, with separate profiles for each property.
  • Role-based access control (admin vs assistant vs accountant) to restrict access based on user roles.
  • Notifications via SMS and email for rent due reminders, ensuring tenants are aware of their payments.
  • Implementation of an automatic rent increase system based on predefined rates or market conditions.
  • Mobile app development to give tenants and property owners full access on the go.
  • Integration with property maintenance tracking to easily request and manage repairs.
  • Real-time analytics and dashboard enhancements for in-depth insights into rental trends and payment history.
  • Support for multi-currency payments for international tenants.
